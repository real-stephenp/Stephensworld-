Delegated: The Cave Adventurer

Flanester Cave... You heard of it when you were but a youngling. It is a cave with a known reputation for being one of the most deadly caves out there. Many have died at the gates, mauled to pieces by the bear that guards it. Even less lay dead in its depths. Folks tell that there are treasures in the cave, but who knows what happens there.

It runs within the Larcron Mountain, across the Flanester river. You find yourself, somehow, trapped within its depths. You must do what you need to do. Adventure, escape, explore, whatever you want to call it. You must do it.

Exploring a large cave full of scary monsters, challenging puzzles, and tons of secrets and treasures! You are delegated, this is Delegated: The Cave Adventurer.

Crow Kid

Do you know that book that I have been obsessing over on this website? The one book, that everybody who reads more than a sentence of this site knows about? Yes, it is the one book that you are thinking of. Yes, it is Crow Kid. The long awaited, one year, long time novel project itself. I have finished it. Here it is, enjoy!

Crow Kid is an urban fantasy story about a young child by the name of Paull William Geraldson. He finds that a very nagging fact lies at his back. He is a shapeshifter, and must move into the forest immediately. He finds that the trip there is hard, and that another crow-shifter is there. Only, they are out to stop them. What will our Paull do? Will he succumb to the everlasting boredom and tire of modern life, or will he walk into his destiny?
